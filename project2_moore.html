Chocolate Buttermilk Cupcakes with Frosting:

Your new go-to recipe for cupcakes!

An easy cupcake recipe for beginner bakers:
Make these super moist and rich cupcakes for your next occassion...or just because.

Top 'em with your favorite frosting and sprinkles.

Yields: 2 dozen medium size cupcakes

Step 1: Ingredients

Have ingredients prepped and ready before you start mixing

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, soften and cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup room temperature water

Step 2: Prep your pans and tools

Before you start baking, make sure you have the correct equipment

  • 2 - 12 cup muffin tins filled with paper liners
  • 2 - medium size mixing bowls
  • Standing kitchen mixer with paddle attachment
  • Liquid measuring cup
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups and measuring spoons
  • Spring-loaded scoop
  • Cooling grid

Step 3: Directions

Start your baking oven and let's mix

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a medium bowl, add 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour, 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Using a whisk, mix the dry ingredients together until it is lump-free. Set aside
  3. In a standing kitchen mixer, cream together 1/2 cup unsalted butter and 1 1/2 cup white sugar until light and fluffy. This will take 8-12 minutes on medium speed
  4. In a medium bowl, whisk together 2 eggs and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. Slowly pour this into the butter-sugar mixture while it is mixing on low speed. Stop the mixer, scrape the sides and paddle attachment with a spatula. Make sure it is uniform!
  5. In a liquid measuring cup, add 1/2 cup buttermilk and 1/2 cup water. Whisk until combined
  6. Start the mixer back on low speed, add the dry ingredients alternately with the buttermilk and water mixture
  7. Stop mixer and scrape down the bowl sides and attachment with a spatula. Stir to ensure the batter is unfiorm
  8. Using a scoop (or a measuring cup if you do not have one), fill the muffin tins 2/3 fill
  9. Bake, in oven, for 20 minutes. Rotating the pan half way after 10 minutes
  10. To ensure it is done baking, insert a tooth pick or paring knife into one of the cupcakes. If it come out clean, it is done. If it does not, bake for 5 more minutes and test again

Step 4: Cool and prep frosting

Let the cupcakes cool

  1. Place the muffin tins on a cooling grid, or heat safe surface
  2. Let it completely cool to room temperature. This will take 2-3 hours
  3. Prep your favorite frosting recipe while the cupcakes are cooling
  4. Below is an easy frosting recipe for beginners

Easy American Frosting Recipe:

  1. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, soften and cubed
  2. 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  3. 2 oz unsweetened chocolated, melted
  4. 2 tablespoons milk, your choice
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. pinch of fine sea salt

Combine all ingredients in a standing kitchen mixer with the paddle attachment. Mix on low speed until smooth, 5-8 minutes. Stop and scrape down with a spatula. Mix again for 5 minutes on medium speed.

Step 5: Decorate

Garnish the cupcakes

  • After the cupcakes have cooled, using an offset spatula to frost the cupcakes
  • Top with your favorite cupcake decorations
  • Popular options: Use rainbow sprinkles, chopped chocolate or nuts
  • Share and enjoy!
